## User Summary

`orchestrating-workflows` ships seven skill-scoped shell helpers (argv partition, findings parser, Decision-Flow resolver, PR-number extractor, new-workflow composite, resume gate, plus a new `set-model-override` subcommand on the existing `workflow-state.sh`) that replace the remaining prose ceremony around model-selection flags, findings ingestion, PR-number resolution, workflow init, and the resume precondition tree. Seven scripts plus SKILL.md + references prose edits cut ~1,500–2,500 tokens of mechanical per-workflow overhead while leaving every judgment-bearing prose surface (chain selection, pause narratives, error-recovery language, dispatch between main/fork/inline) untouched. The pre-fork ceremony script (item 9.2 / `prepare-fork.sh`) is already shipped as FEAT-021 and is not in scope.
