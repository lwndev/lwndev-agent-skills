## User Summary

Reduce output tokens generated by the `orchestrating-workflows` skill by adding an explicit *Output style* directive, formalizing the fork-to-orchestrator return-format contract (`done | artifact=<path> | <note>`, `failed | <reason>`), and trimming conversational narration. The pilot measures token/line savings before and after, captures learnings, and files a rollout issue for the remaining twelve `lwndev-sdlc` skills. Scope is limited to `SKILL.md` and its two reference files; artifact templates in sub-skills are deferred to the rollout.
